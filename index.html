Commands

=help - List of commands.

=play <query> - Plays audio from a URL or search for requested song name.

=skip - Skips currents song/stream.

=stop - Stop the current song.

=resume - Resumes the current song.

=queue - Displays all of the songs that is queued.

=np - Displays currently played song/radio.

=vol <number> - Sets the volume of the bot for everyone in channel (number between 1 and 100).

=clear - Clears the queue.

=repeat - Repeat Current Song.
